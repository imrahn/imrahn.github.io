Who We Are

Deerfoot Bottle Depot wants you to help combat global warming and keeping the environment litter free by recycling your bottles. From milk containers and aluminium cans to beer and wine bottles, we will pay you for your recycled products. We specialize in bottle drives and create efficient solutions to recycle used boxes, milk jugs, bottles, cans, and aluminium.

Our Committment

Deerfoot Bottle Depot is a recycling corporation that strives to create solutions to reduce litter and greenhouse gas emmissions through recycling bottles and various containers.

Charities

Deerfoot Bottle Depot in Calgary works with a wide variety of charities and organizations (such as the Canadian Diabetes Association) to help raise money for their causes. Whether you'd like to donate your containers to help support a cause, or you're interested in setting up a donation program, we can help.

What Can You Return?

In Alberta, the majority of ready-to-drink beverages are included, with the exception of oil-based coffee creamer containers. When you finish a carton of milk or a bottle of juice, don’t throw the container into the trash! You’ve paid a deposit on that container, and we’ll refund that deposit when you recycle with Deerfoot Bottle Depot.

How Much Is Each Container Worth?

All beverage containers in Alberta are now accepted at bottle depots for a refund of 10 cent or 25 cent deposit paid on them in stores. One Litre and less will return 10 cents and over one Litre will return 25 cents!
